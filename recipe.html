The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is the best sandwich you is ever going to taste, just look at it. If that doesn't remind you of your crush I don't know what will.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board
  • A stove
  • A Pan

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of the bread next to each other on the cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 2/8 inch layer of peanut butter on of the slices of bead
  3. On the other slice you are goin to spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. Heat up the pan in the stove
  5. Carefully place the 2 slices of bread together
  6. Place the sandwich in the pan until it turns golden in both sides
